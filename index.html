Fish Bowl

Watch the fish swim endless lap. Laps completed: 0

How it works

  • The fish swim around an invisible circular path inside the bowl.
  • Each full revolution increments the lap counter at the top.
  • Use the controls to pause, change speed, or reset.
  • Try hovering or clicking the fish for a little bounce!

Pro tip: This demo uses requestAnimationFrame, an SVG scene, and a bit of trigonometry to keep things smooth and accurate.
